Dicer


Welcome to the Portfolio website of the Dicer application.


What is Dicer?

Dicer is a social media meet-up app for people, veterans and newcomers alike, to quickly find other people to play Dungeons and Dragons with.


How does Dicer work?

This is what anyone who wanted to use Dicer would do:

  1. Log in or sign up. New players will be guided through creating a Player or DM profile.
  2. Search for other users based on everything important to DnD.
  3. Find users and message them. You can make friends with them and talk more in chat channels.
  4. You can also use Facebook to find friends of yours that use Dicer and add them as your friends on-site.
  5. Go out and play DnD!
